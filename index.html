Sir Felix's Information Repo

What is this?

This is a website that I made to try and contribute somewhat to mostly the Homestuck and by extent CaNWC community putting up pages for information to be accessible to most people, and also to just mess around and see what goofy stuff I can make that someone stumbling across this site might enjoy.

What is mostly hosted here?

Well, I'm glad you asked. Here's a current directory of what is currently accessible on the site:
  • CaNWC Direct Page for September 23rd, 2016. A page for watching the most recent Homestuck / CaNWC Community Streams.
  • CaNWC NX Direct Page for September 13th, 2016. It was a bad joke...
  • CaNWC Direct Page for September 3rd, 2016.
  • CaNWC Direct Archive Page. Mainly a page for past CaNWC Music Team streams and videos and other misc resources.
  • Sir Felix's old stupid personal webpage that you shouldn't bother with.
There will most likely be more pages in the future, so keep watch for those.
